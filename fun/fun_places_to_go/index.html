San Francisco

Things to do

  1. golden gate bridge
  2. golden gate park
  3. Lombard street 九曲花街
  4. fishman's wharf 渔人码头
  5. cable car 铛铛车
  6. alcatraz island 恶魔岛
  7. union square
  8. twin peaks 双子峰
  9. San Jose, 1hour south
  10. Stanford, 40min south
  11. 17 mile drive, 1.5+ hour south from downtown, pass by San Jose (silicon valley)
  12. High way 1, about 3 hour south from downtown,

Near by national parks

  1. redwood national park, 5 hour north
  2. Yosemite national park, 3hr east

suggested plan

  • ride the cable car from downtown to Fishman's wharf
  • rent a bike at fishman's wharf and ride to golden gate bridge, bike on the bridge
  • bike back to fishman's wharf.
  • OR, bike all the way to Sausalito Ferry terminal and take the Ferry back to fishman's wharf. It would be very tiring this way

Chicago

A 2-day trip plan

  • Day 1:
    1. Art Institute of Chicago
    2. Cloud Gate
    3. Millennium Park
    4. Skydeck Chicago
    5. Museum of Science and Industry - this is near the University
  • Day 2:
    1. The Field Museum
    2. University of Chicago
    3. Michigan Avenue
    4. Magnificent Mile

Optional

  • Wrigley Field
  • The Boring Store - It just sells really funny things

Food

  1. The Parthenon - Greek food, Chicago has a great greek town. I think most of their restaurants are pretty good
  2. Purple pig - Tapas, sometimes has long lines
  3. chicago style hotdogs if you like hotdogs - probably many places to choose from
  4. Deep Dish Pizza - I have only been to Uno's but that is a chain. I heard that Lou Malnatis is good.
  5. the chinatown there is better than Seattle's but not better than Vancouver
  6. Clarks - this is a 24 hour diner. Its a chicago chain, pretty famous, and a fun experience but the food is just what you would expect from a diner - large portions for low prices!
  7. Hot Chocolate - nice desserts
