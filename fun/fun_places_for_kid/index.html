Fun Seattle places for 6 month old

  • Gymboree play & learn 2 on Tuesday 1:15-2:00pm

    Gymboree Play & Music, Ballard
    4609 14th Ave. NW, #103, Seattle, WA 98107 United States
    (206) 783-3741

  • Infant Co-ops at NSC
  • Baby story time at Seattle Puclic Library

    10:15 – 10:45 a.m. on every Saturday
    Where: Northgate Branch 10548 5th Ave NE, Seattle, WA 98125
    Room: Meeting Room
    Summary: It is baby story time at the Northgate Branch! Bring your infants age 0-12 months to enjoy stories, rhymes, and songs with our childrens librarian. Stay afterward for a social playtime with other babies.

  • Story time at Mountlake Terrace Library

    Event Type: Storytime
    Age Group(s): Preschool
    Day: every Thursday
    Start Time: 10:00 AM
    Description: Babies and their favorite adults learn to love reading together with stories, songs, fingerplays and rhymes. Playtime follows. For ages 3 months to 2 years. Caregiver required.
    Address: 23300 58th Ave W, Mountlake Terrace, WA 98043
    Phone:(425) 776-8722

  • Story time at Lynnwood Library

    Event Type: Family Program
    Age Group(s): Preschool
    Day: every Tuesday
    Start Time: 10:00 AM
    End Time: 10:45 AM
    Description: Wiggle and giggle with your baby through silly stories, happy songs, rhymes, and activities that inspire a love of reading. For newborns through 18 months. Caregiver required.
    Library: Lynnwood Library, 19200 44th Ave W, Lynnwood, WA 98036
    Location: Lynnwood Large Mtg. Room
